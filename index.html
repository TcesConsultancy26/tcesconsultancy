About TCES Consultancy

TCES Consultancy – The Complete Employment Solution was established in 2026 as a professionally managed manpower supply and recruitment consultancy. We are committed to providing reliable manpower solutions, labour contract services, and placement support to industries and organizations across Odisha.

Our head office is located at At-Loisingha, District Balangir, Odisha – 767020. We proudly serve clients across Balangir, Bargarh, Sambalpur, Jharsuguda, Sonepur, Rayagada, Kalahandi, Kandhamal, Nuapada, Malkangiri, Sundargarh, Koraput, Boudh, and other districts of Odisha.

Our dedicated team specializes in Administration, Human Resources, Recruitment, Marketing, Business Development, Site Operations, and Legal & Statutory Compliance. We provide skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled manpower, including Graduates, Diploma Engineers, ITI candidates, Housekeeping Staff, Executive Drivers, Supervisors, and Industrial Workforce.

Our mission is to deliver quality manpower services with professionalism, integrity, and customer satisfaction while building long-term relationships with both employers and job seekers.
